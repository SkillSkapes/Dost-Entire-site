    Institute Admission

    The following Information Brochure has detailed information and fee structures for the incoming batch (UG/PG 20XX-20YY).

    Certificate Originals

    Please bring originals of the following on admission day:
    • Roll number
    • Offer of Admission Letter
    • Birth Certificate
    • 10th standard mark list/pass certificate
    • 12th standard mark list/pass certificate
    • Admit card for JEE Advanced/HSEE/GATE/relevant entrance exam
    • Community certificate for SC/ST/OBC candidates (as applicable)
    • Certificate for Person with Disability candidates (as applicable)
    • Certificate for Environmentally Weaker Section candidates (as applicable)

    Forms

    The forms required for admission will have been sent to you along with your offer letter. They are also in the annexures of the Information Brochure attached above. Please fill out all the applicable ones and bring them to campus on admission day.
    • Proof of tuition fee payment
    • Medical examination report (please note: this is distinct from the JOSAA-required medical report)
    • Scholarship application forms and corresponding income certificates, as applicable
