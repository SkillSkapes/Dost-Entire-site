    Meet the Dean

    Prof. Nilesh J Vasa has been serving as Dean (Students) since 2020. He is a faculty in the department of Engineering Design and his research focuses on laser applications. He holds a Doctorate in Engineering from Kyushu University, Fukoka.

    In his capacity as Dean (Students), Prof. Vasa is responsible for the administration of hostels, student cultural and sports activities, and many other facilities.
