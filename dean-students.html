The Dean (Students)

Prof. Nilesh J Vasa has been serving as the 13th Dean (Students) since 2020. He is a faculty in the department of Engineering Design and his research focuses on laser applications. He holds a Doctorate in Engineering from Kyushu University, Fukoka.

In his capacity as Dean (Students), Prof. Vasa is responsible for the administration of hostels, and student activities such as sporting events, cultural activities, and publications. He is also responsible for the maintenance of student discipline. He chairs the Board of Students, a Board that formulates policies for the conduct of extra-curricular activities and the functioning of hostels subject to the approval of the Senate
